Self-made Vanilla Root Bitter*

Preparation Time: 3 hours+ | Yield: about 165ml
Recipe Ratio
① 10g Vanilla Root
② 200ml Angel Fungus Original Bitter
Production Process
Production Process Equipment & Tools Used
○ Cut 10g of vanilla root into 5mm small pieces with scissors, place in a vacuum bag (note: vacuum bag size must be appropriate), then add 200ml Angel Fungus Original Bitter, seal well; ① Vacuum Machine
○ Put the vacuum bag in a low-temperature slow cooker water bath, heat at 65°C for 3 hours; ② Low-temp Slow Cooker
○ After completion, remove the vacuum bag and let it cool to room temperature, then filter with coffee filter paper to extract the bitter; ③ Scissors
○ After bottling, label with production date (bitter has high cost, make less, suggest liquid doesn't exceed too many measuring cups, because each time passing through measuring cup, it will absorb a bit). ④ Coffee Filter Paper
⑤ Electronic Scale
